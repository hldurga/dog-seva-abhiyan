🐕 Our Mission

Dog Seva Abhiyan is a grassroots movement driven by compassion. From feeding over 100 stray dogs daily to providing emergency medical care and vaccinations, our team of volunteers is committed to creating a better world for voiceless animals.

  • 🥣 Daily Feeding Drives
  • 💉 Vaccination & Treatment
  • 📢 Awareness & Adoption Campaigns
  • 🤝 Community Involvement
